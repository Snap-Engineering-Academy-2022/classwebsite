Released on August 23rd of 2013, Six feet beneath the moon is UK based King Krule's first studio album. Archy Marshall, the man man behind King Krule, the singer an songwriter makes unexpected waves with his work and it especially shines in this album. Often there aren't many things going in with the instrumentation, but yet King Krule manages to make these songs roar with life. One of the shining aspects of his songs is his vocals! King Krule's distinct, loud, raw, and bass heavy voice ripples through tracks flooding with power and emotion surely to peak ears.
