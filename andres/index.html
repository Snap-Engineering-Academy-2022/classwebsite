Six feet beneath the moon

Released on August 23rd of 2013, Six feet beneath the moon is UK based King Krule's debut studio album. Archy Marshall, the man behind King Krule, the singer and songwriter makes unexpected waves with his work and it especially shines in this album. Often there aren't many things going in with the instrumentation in the album, but yet King Krule manages to make these songs roar with life. One of the shining aspects of his songs are his vocals! King Krule's distinct, loud, raw, and bass heavy voice ripples through tracks flooding with power and emotion surely to peak ears.
